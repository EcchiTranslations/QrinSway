This is a free, unofficial translation by anons that like the ENF genre, and do this for love of it. We ask that you support the author by purchasing the game instead of pirating it. They are already aware of this translation, and we don't want to cause any further problems. We want to show that the English community, however small it is, has their support and would like to see more.
